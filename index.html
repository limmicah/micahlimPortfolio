Fantasy in C.

[2026]

A fantasia (or fantasy) is a free-form musical composition that does not adhere to strict structural conventions. Reflecting this idea, Fantasy in C. unfolds as a musical train of thought. It begins with a bright fanfare and gradually moves through shifting colors and territories, at times moving jaggedly and at others flowing smoothly. The music frequently changes direction, altering its perspective throughout the piece, and is filled with both unexpected turns and subtle, reflective moments.
